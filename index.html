What do these numbers mean?

Score (0-100)
Facebook's ML model assigns each friend a base affinity (feature 16173) reflecting how likely you are to interact with them. We normalise this to 0-100 relative to your own social graph, where 100 = your closest friend.
Raw Base
The original affinity value straight from Facebook's payload. The range is narrow (typically ~0.389-0.414) because it's a probability-like weight, but small differences matter at scale.
Active
Detects a structural +0.5 engagement bonus in the multiplier (feature 16174). "YES" means Facebook's system considers you actively engaging with this person — messaging, reacting, or visiting their profile.
